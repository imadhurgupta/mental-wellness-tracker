# Mental Wellness Tracker

A mental wellness tracker application designed to support students preparing for high-stakes competitive exams (like JEE, NEET, UPSC) by fostering a supportive community and providing tools for stress management.

## Overview
Many students experience intense stress and isolation during exam preparation. This app provides a safe, anonymous space for students to share their struggles, receive peer support, and access expert-curated resources to manage anxiety and build resilience.

## Features
- **Anonymous Sharing**: Post stress levels, challenges, or venting moments without revealing identity.
- **Peer Support**: Receive supportive reactions and messages from peers who understand the struggle.
- **Expert Guidance**: Access short audio clips (1-3 minutes) from mental health professionals tailored to exam-related anxiety.
- **Calm-First Design**: Soothing color palette, ample whitespace, and anxiety-reducing interface elements.
- **Privacy-Focused**: Local storage for sensitive data and anonymous profiles to protect user privacy.
